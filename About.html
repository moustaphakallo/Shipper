What we do

Our main goals for business

We built Shipper around one job: getting cargo from point A to point B without surprises. Everything below exists to make that promise reliable, not just possible.

CARGO / 01

Sea Freight

Full and part-container loads booked across major shipping lines, with consolidated cargo options for smaller volumes.

CARGO / 02

Air Freight

Time-critical and high-value shipments routed through priority air lanes when sea transit isn't fast enough.

CARGO / 03

Road Freight

First- and last-mile haulage that connects ports and airports to warehouses, depots, and final delivery addresses.

CARGO / 04

Other Freight

Rail, multimodal, and specialized transport for oversized, temperature-sensitive, or otherwise non-standard cargo.

CARGO / 05

Warehousing

Short- and long-term storage, with inventory management, order fulfillment, and cross-docking services.

CARGO / 06

Customs & Logistics

Supply chain planning, packaging, and documentation services that keep shipments moving without regulatory or customs delays.
